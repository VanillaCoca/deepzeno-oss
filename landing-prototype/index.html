FAQ

Questions, answered.

Who is ZENO for?
Anyone running a real, evolving inquiry that's important enough to research properly and open-ended enough to change shape as you learn — money, a move abroad, a career turn, a big purchase, a business idea, a research question. It rewards people who want to think rigorously, not just get a quick answer.
How is this different from Perplexity or ChatGPT?
Those answer one question at a time and forget the rest. ZENO is built around the project — a living structure of topics, findings, and confirmed judgment that persists across sessions and holds together even when your goal turns 180°.
Does it decide things for me?
No. ZENO researches, structures, and surfaces the assumptions worth a second look — you decide what's true. Nothing becomes project truth without your explicit confirmation. Its autonomy is read-only.
What happens when my goal changes?
That's the point. The project catches a vague idea and follows it wherever it turns. Earlier branches stay connected, and what you've confirmed keeps accumulating — so you never re-derive it and the thread never breaks.
I'm a developer — can it feed my coding agents?
Yes. The same confirmed project truth is exposed to Claude Code, Cursor, or Codex via MCP, candidate-first: agents propose, you confirm. But that's a secondary, downstream path — ZENO is a research workspace first.
